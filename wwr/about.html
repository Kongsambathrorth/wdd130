Rapids! Make Memories!

The sport of whitewater rafting was in its infant stages in 1962. That spring George Armstrong placed a small raft into the Stanislaus River near Yosemite National Park and ventured into the unknown. He lived to tell about it and fell in love with rivers and rafting.

History

More than 60 years of rafting on California rivers is enough time to experience anything that can happen on a whitewater rafting trip. Organizing over 40,000 trips is the best way to learn how to put on amazing rafting adventures! Taking 450,000 guests down rivers is a great way to learn how to care for folks as you lead them through river canyons.
