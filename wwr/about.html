Dry Oar White Water Rafting

Our Mission and Vision

At Dry Oar, we believe that adventure changes lives. Our mission is to provide safe, unforgettable white water rafting experiences that connect people with the natural beauty of the river while upholding the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship.

History

The company was founded in **1985** by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share the thrill of whitewater with everyone. Starting with just two rafts and a beat-up van, we've grown into the premier outfitter in the region, known for our commitment to safety, conservation, and fun. We believe that everyone should experience the power and beauty of the river at least once.
