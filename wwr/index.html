Our Heritage

A Passion for Rivers Since '98

River Rush Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river enthusiasts dedicated to sharing the thrill of whitewater with the world. Our business intent is simple: to provide a truly thrilling and remarkably safe experience for every single guest.

We operate on the belief that a well-organized trip, top-notch gear, and highly trained guides are the foundation of a great adventure. We are committed to environmental stewardship, helping to preserve the free-flowing rivers that make our trips possible.
